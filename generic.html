Dashboard


This Dashboard shows interconnected fields which analyses various aspects that goes into Jobs of Data Science.

Various factors such as Average salaries in USD by experience level and employment type, Total companies by size and location, Average salary by countries, Top 10 employee residence, Experience level, Employment type, and last but not the least Average salary by job title and experience level can all be customly viewed according to viewer's choice by using the filters on the visualisation on the basis of Company Location and Job Title.
